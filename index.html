Tianao Ge

I am a PhD student at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), under the supervision of Prof. Hongyuan Liu. My research interests are in optimizing compilers and high-performance computing.

Before I joined HKUST-GZ, I graduated from Sun Yat-sen University with a master's degree in 2022, advised by Prof. Xianwei Zhang. And I received my B.Eng. degree from Wuhan University of Technology in 2020.
